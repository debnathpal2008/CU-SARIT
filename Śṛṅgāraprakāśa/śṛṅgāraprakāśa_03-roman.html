na caivamākhyātāntarasthaṃ viśeṣaṇamākhyātamabhivyañjayitumabhidhātuṃ vā śaknoti, dvayorapi prādhānyena parasparānapekṣatvāt | bhavatu copasargāderasattvārthasyāpi nīlotpalādivadeva sāmānādhikaraṇyena viśeṣaṇaviśeṣyabhāvaḥ; vaiyadhikaraṇye tu vyatirekāpekṣāyāṃ kathamivaitadbhavati ? na hi aparinippannatvenātmalābha eva kṣīṇasyāparopakāriṇaḥ karaṇādayo vyatirekā yujyante | ato vaiyadhikaraṇyenāpi na tiṅantayorviśeṣaṇaviśeṣyabhāvassambandho bhavati | sādhyasādhanabhāvo 'pi nopapadyate, sa hi kriyayoḥ kartṛtve karmatve vā syāt; sādhyaṃ hi vastūtpadyamānatvena kartā karma ceti | na caitadubhayamapi kriyāyāḥ kriyāntaraṃ prati yujyate | tiṅantena hi kriyātmotpattiṃ prati prayatamānakārakākāṅkṣāpi prakṛtyā prayogeṣu viniveśitasvarūpā 'bhidhīyate | ato 'pratibdhātmarūpatvena kriyāyāssādhanaparavaśatvena tadavaruddhatvāditareṇa kriyātmanā sambandhasattvenāsyāḥ kartṛrūpatā karmarūpatā vā sambhavati, yataḥ pariniṣpannarūpasyātmalābhanirapekṣasya siddhātmanassādhanābhāvo ghaṭate, na tūktaviparītātmana iti | kriyāyāś‍cāyameva svabhāvaḥ, yaduta sādhanādhīnatvena sādhyasvabhāvāyāḥ pratītiḥ | yā ca sādhyasvabhāvā sā kathamivānyasmin sādhye sādhanabhāvaṃ gacchet, yena kartrītyucyate ? yā ca sādhanādhīnatvena sādhyasvabhāvā sā kathamiva kriyā sādhyeta, yena karmetyucyate ? yadi ca syāt pariniṣpannarūpadravyavadevedaṃtayā nirdiśyeta | na ca nirdiśyate, ato 'nidantayā nirdeśādapi na sādhanam | avasthārūpaś‍ca sādhanadharmo vyāpāraśabdavācyaḥ kriyā | sā kathamivātmasiddhaye tathābivadhameva dharmāntaramapekṣeta ? anapekṣitayoś‍ca kathamiva mithassambandhopapattiḥ ?

na ca kriyāyāḥ kimapi tadavaśiṣyate yatsādhanairasādhitaṃ kriyāntarasya sādhyaṃ bhaviṣyati | tadevamitaretarānāyattarūpayorasambandha eva kriyayoḥ | na ca nirvartamānalabdhātmakaṃ vastu nirvartakādṛte pareṇa saha sambandhamarhet | upakāryopakārakanibandhanasvabhāvānāmitaretareṇa saṃbandhalabdhātmakasya copakārakatvam | kriyā ca tathārūpatvavirahāt sādhanebhyassvotpattimavekṣamāṇā kathamivātathābhūtakriyāntareṇa saṃbandhamiyāt ? tasmāt svasādhanāvaruddhatvāt sādhyamānaikasvabhāvāyāḥ kriyāyāḥ kartṛtve karmatve vā na kriyāntareṇa saha saṃbandhaḥ |

nanu ca yathā pākaṃ karoti, pāko vartate iti tiṅkṛdabhihitayoḥ kriyayossādhyasādhanabhāvo dṛśyate, tathā ca paṭhati pacatītyetayorapi kasmānna bhavati ? naivam; uktamidaṃ bhāṣye, ‘asti khalu tiṅabhihitasya kṛdabhihitasya bhāvasya viśeṣaḥ, [page 172] kṛdabhihito hi bhāvo dravyavat prakāśamānaḥ kriyayā samavāyaṃ gacchati’ iti | na hi bhavati pacati paṭhati iti lakṣyalakṣaṇabhāvo 'pyākhyātābhihitayoḥ kriyayorabhinnasādhanayorvā saṃbhavati | dvidhāpi cānupapattiḥ; sādhyamānatvena pravartamānasya sādhanaparatantrasya cātmano 'nidantānirdeśasya sādhanavallakṣaṇatvāyogāt | nanu ca mā bhūt kriyāyāssādhanam; lakṣaṇaṃ tūbhayathāpi dṛśyate, pācakaḥ paṭhati, rudataḥ prāvrājīditi | tato yaḥ pacati sa paṭhati, sa roditi, ayaṃ pravrajatītyākhyātayorlakṣyalakṣaṇabhāvo bhaviṣyati | ciraṃ jīva, tvayaiva smārito 'smi | ākhyātayorlakṣyalakṣaṇatvenāsambandhakāraṇasya yadatra ‘lakṣaṇahetvoḥ kriyāyāḥ’ (3.2.126) iti śatṛśānacau tiṅapavādau smaryete | ato lakṣaṇatve kriyāyāḥ kriyāntaraṃ prati samāśrīyamāṇe pacan paṭhati, rudataḥ prāvrājīdityeva bhavitavyam |

nanu yaḥ pacati sa paṭhati, sa rodityayaṃ pravrajatīti tatkathaṃ sambandhaḥ? etena hetuhetumadbhāvaḥ ākhyātayoḥ pratyukto bhavati, yato 'sāvapi dvidhā— heturvā hetumatparatvena, hetumato vā hetuparatvena | tatra hetorhetumatparatve śatṛśānajbhyāṃ hetumatassvahetuparatve tumunādibhirbhāvyam | yathā pacan bhuṅkte, adhīyamāno vasati, bhoktuṃ pacati, bhojako vrajatīti | nanu pacati, bhuṅkte, vrajatīti tat kasya kena saṃbandhaḥ ? avadhyavadhimadbhāvo 'pi tiṅntayornāstyeva | sa hi deśataḥ kālato vā syāt | na tāvaddeśataḥ, tasya mūrtimadviṣayatvāt | kālatastu yujyate, kiṃtu tatra pūrvakāle ktvāpratyayavidhānāt paktvā bhuṅkta ityeva syāt, na tu pacati bhuṅkta iti | samuccayenāpi kriyāyāssaṃbandhaḥ | so 'pi hi dvidhaiva; sādhanānāṃ vā kriyāyāṃ yathā gauraśvaś‍ca gṛhyatām; kriyāṇāṃ vā sādhane yathā asau pacati paṭhati ceti; na tu kriyāṇāṃ kriyāyāmeva |

nanu caikatvāt kriyāyā yadyapi sādhanasamuccaye kriyābhisaṃbandho nāsti, tathāpyekavastuviṣayatvena kriyāsamuccaye 'sau bhaviṣyati | tathā hi— ya eva pacati sa eva paṭhatītyaikādhikaraṇye bhavatyeva sambandhopapattiḥ | naivaṃ sādhyayossādhanāyattayoralabdhātmanossādhanonmukhatvena parivartamānayoḥ, kriyāyāḥ parasparānapekṣatvāt; na hyananvitātmanāmadhikaraṇamapekṣāmupajanayitumalam | atassavyetaragoviṣāṇavat paraspareṇāsamanvitameva kriyādvayaṃ sādhane samuccīyateṃ na ca samuccīyamāne vastunī mithassaṃbadhyete, tathā sati samāhāretaretarayoranyatarāpattau samuccayasyaivābhāvāt | ādigrahaṇādanvācaye 'pyananvaya eva kriyayoḥ | tasmānnāstyevākhyātayormithassaṃbandha iti |

nanu ca asti sa me rocate, asti tvamīśiṣe, nāhaṃ rame 'smi iti sāmānādhikaraṇyena viśeṣaṇaviśeṣyabhāvaḥ; bhavati pacati, bhavati pakṣyati, bhavatyapākṣīt, bhavedapi bhavet, syādapi syāditi kartṛtvena; paś‍ya mṛgo dhāvati, iha paś‍yāmaḥ karmaṇi dvinacanabahuvacanānyudāhriyante, ehi manye rathena yāsyasi, smarasi devadatta kaś‍mīreṣu vatsyāmastatraudanaṃ bhokṣyāmahe, na marṣayāmi yattatra bhavān vṛṣalaṃ yājayediti karmatvena sādhyasādhanabhāvaḥ; yo bhuṅkte sa gṛhyatām, yo 'dhīte sa pacatītyabhinnasādhanatvena; sa svapiti eṣa budhyate, sa udeti ayamastametīti bhinnasādhanatvena lakṣyalakṣaṇabhāvaḥ; hantīti palāyate, varṣatīti dhāvatīti hetorhetumatparatvena; vakṣyāmīti vrajati, bhokṣyate ityāsta iti hetuhetumatorhetuparatvena hetuhetumadbhāvaḥ; yāvadbhuṅkte tato vrajati, yadayaṃ bhuṅkte tataḥ paṭhati, yadayaṃ bhuk‍tvā vrajatītyadhīta eva tataḥ paramiti avadhyavadhimadbhāvaḥ; sak‍tūn piba, dhānāḥ khāda, odanaṃ bhuṅkṣvetyevāyamabhyavaharati, pacati cāyaṃ paṭhati ceti samuccayaḥ; vaṭo! | bhikṣāmaṭa gāṃ cānayetvanvācayo 'pi tiṅanteṣu dṛś‍yate | tatkathamucyate nākhyātayossaṃbandho 'stīti?

naivam; vāk‍yabhedādinā tadupapatteḥ | asti sa sa me rocate, asi tvaṃ tvamīśiṣe, asmyahamahaṃ rame, astyetad gacchāmo vayaṃ, bhavatvevametat payasodhayāmayatvamamithyevāsi (?), yanmurajitaṃ tvamapūpuja ityukteṣu vāk‍yabhedā upapadyante | nipātā vā astyādayaḥ ākhyātasarūpā nirarthakā api vāk‍yapūraṇāya kamīmidvādivatprayujyante; na tu kriyābhirviśeṣaṇaviśeṣyabhāvamanubhavanti | bhavati pacatītyādiṣu tu pacatīti pakṣyatīti apakṣīditi ca yo 'rtho bhavati asāvastītyarthaḥ | nanu pacatyāderbhavatau kartṛtvena sādhyasādhanabhāvaḥ; evaṃ bhavedapi bhavet, syādapi syāditi |

athavā yo dhāvati mṛgastaṃ paśryeti sarvanāmnā yadā so 'rthaḥ pratyavamṛṣṭo bhavati tadā kriyā tasyopalakṣaṇāya pravartate | yo dhāvati sartāraṃ taṃ paśryati; ayaṃ cārthaḥ paśrya dhāvatīti padayorasaṃbandhatvena śrutārthāpattigamyaḥ na tu śabdasanniveśī |

athavā dve ete vāk‍ye—ekametāvati vāk‍yam ‘iha paśryāmaḥ’ karmaṇi dvivacanabahuvacanādīni vacanānyeva dṛśaḥ karmatvenāvatiṣṭhante, nodāhṛtiḥ | ataś‍ca paśryāmo yasmādudāhriyante prayujyanta iti dvitīyam | ehi manye rathena yāsyasītyatra ‘prahāse ca manyopapade manyateruttama ekavacca’ (1.4.106) iti vacanād yadāyamarthaḥ ehi manyase rathena yāsyāmīti | yadā vā sūtrapratiṣedhapakṣe svaviṣaya eva madhyamottamau tadā sarvathā gamyamānetiśabdopasaṃhṛtasya vāk‍yārthasyaiva karmatvam | ehīti ca prahāsaviṣayasaṃbodhanārtha ākhyātasvarūpo nipātaḥ | ākhyāṃtasvarūpāś‍ca puruṣaṃ saṃkhyāṃ na vyabhicaranti; na hi bhavati tvamasti vayamastīti | kiñcāstirapi nipātaḥ; so 'yaṃ yathā astikṣīrā gauḥ; tatra hyākhyātamiti kṛtvoktaḥ; subadhikāre 'stikṣīrādivacanamiti; na cāvyayatvāt; nipāto 'yamiti parihāraḥ |

nanu ca saṅkhyāmavyabhicaran kathamekaśabdo bahūnāṃ prahāse bahutvaṃ brūyāt ? naivam; bahubhirekaḥ prahasyate, naikena bahavaḥ, na hi bahuṣvekaṃ prahasatsu sūtrapratiṣedhapakṣe ‘ehi manye rathena yāsyāmi’ (iti) | yadā vā sūtrapratiṣedhapakṣe astīti kathaṃ na sattvabhūto 'rthassādhyasādhanasaṃbandhamanubhavati ? yo bhuṅkte sa gṛhyatāmityatrāpi vāk‍yadvitayameva | na cātra kriyayoḥ lakṣyalakṣaṇabhāvaḥ; kriyāyāḥ kārakopalakṣaṇatvāt | tathā hi yo bhuṅkte sa devadatta iti devadattasattvopalakṣaṇameko vāk‍yārthaḥ; sa tathābhūto gṛhyatāmiti tasya grahaṇasaṃbandhaḥ |

darśanamaparam— na ca yathā sattvaṃ tadrūpābhidhāyitvena smaraṇāt kartṛprādhānyamabhidadhat kriyāyāḥ kriyāntaraṃ prati lakṣaṇatvamabhidhatte, tathā sarvanāmābhidhātuṃ śaknoti | tena hi sā [page 175] kriyā sādhyatvātpracyāvya sādhanopasarjanabhāvamupanītā tatparāmṛṣṭaṃ devadattamupalakṣayati; sa tu tathābhūtaḥ kriyāntareṇa saṃbadhyata iti | na hyekasyāḥ pravṛttessādhanopalakṣaṇamaparakriyāsaṃbandhaś‍copapanno bhavati |
